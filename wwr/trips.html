Our Trips

Rapid Adventure

This trip is designed for thrill-seekers looking to conquer intense rapids. It offers an exhilarating whitewater rafting experience, often featuring Class III-IV rapids that challenge paddlers with fast-moving currents and technical maneuvers. Whether you're navigating the fierce waters of Nepal or another adventurous river, this trip is perfect for those who crave adrenaline while enjoying breathtaking natural landscapes.

Whitewater Extreme

As the name suggests, this trip is for expert-level rafters who want to push their limits. It typically involves Class IV-V rapids, requiring precise teamwork and quick reflexes to navigate turbulent waters. Expect steep drops, powerful waves, and a high-energy ride through rugged river terrain. This trip is ideal for experienced rafters looking for a heart-pounding adventure.

Scenic Float

Unlike the high-intensity rafting trips, this one is calm and relaxing. It takes you through gentle waters, allowing you to soak in stunning views of the surrounding landscapes. Perfect for families, beginners, or those who simply want to enjoy a peaceful river journey, this trip often includes opportunities to spot wildlife and learn about the area's ecology from knowledgeable guides.
